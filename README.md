## üìù Task-wise Analysis

### ‚úÖ Task 1: Data Preprocessing and Cleaning
- Removed duplicate post records
- Standardized date and platform formats
- Ensured numeric fields (likes, impressions, ad spend, downloads) were correctly formatted
- Split multi-hashtag columns into individual hashtag entries

---

### ‚úÖ Task 2: Engagement Analysis
- Calculated **Engagement Rate** per post and platform  
  *(Likes + Comments + Shares) / Impressions*
- Identified **Top 10 posts** by engagement rate
- Created Pivot Tables showing:
  - Total Likes, Comments, and Shares by Content Type and Platform
  - Average Clicks per post by Hashtag
- Ranked and highlighted **top-performing hashtags**

---

### ‚úÖ Task 3: Platform Performance Analysis
- Identified platforms generating the highest engagement
- Compared weekly follower growth across platforms
- Analyzed the relationship between **Ad Spend and Engagement**
- Provided data-driven platform focus recommendations

---

### ‚úÖ Task 4: Hashtag & Content Strategy
- Identified most frequently used hashtags
- Calculated average engagement per hashtag
- Compared performance of content types (Text, Story, Reel) across platforms
- Recommended optimal content type‚Äìplatform combinations

---

### ‚úÖ Task 5: Campaign Effectiveness
- Calculated total and average impressions, likes, and clicks per campaign
- Measured engagement uplift during vs before campaigns
- Identified:
  - Campaign with the highest **ROI (Engagement / Ad Spend)**
  - Campaign driving maximum follower growth

---

### ‚úÖ Task 6: Follower Retention & Loyalty
- Identified the week with the highest net follower gain
- Used moving averages to analyze follower trends
- Analyzed correlation between:
  - Ad Spend vs Follower Growth
  - Ad Spend vs App Downloads

---

## üìà Key Insights
